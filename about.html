About JoPass

We're on a mission to make Jordan's best experiences accessible, affordable, and easy to book.

Our Story
Born in Jordan, built for Jordan

JoPass was created to solve a simple problem — great venues in Jordan often struggle to fill their capacity, while customers want to explore more but feel held back by full-price commitments.

We built a platform that bridges that gap. Venue owners list their best deals and available slots, and customers book them using a simple credits system. One credit, one Jordanian Dinar — no hidden fees, no subscriptions, no complexity.

Whether it's a morning yoga class in Abdoun, a spa afternoon in Sweifieh, or dinner at a hidden gem in downtown Amman — JoPass is your key to more of Jordan.
