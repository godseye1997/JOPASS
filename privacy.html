Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how JoPass collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform.

What We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account or use JoPass:

• Account information — your name, email address, and phone number.

• Booking history — records of services you have booked through the platform.

• Payment information — credit pack purchases. We do not store full card details.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Process and manage your bookings.

• Send session reminders and booking confirmations.

• Improve the JoPass platform and user experience.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (supabase.com) and is retained only as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

Push Notifications

JoPass may send push notifications for booking reminders and updates. Notifications are optional and can be disabled at any time from your device settings.

Cookies & Local Storage

JoPass uses local storage on your device to maintain your session and preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Access the personal data we hold about you.

• Request correction of inaccurate data.

• Request deletion of your account and data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at jopasscc@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

JoPass is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of JoPass after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at jopasscc@gmail.com.
